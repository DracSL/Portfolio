Rock Paper Scissors in Real Time

Crane stance beats Cobra stance

Tiger stance beats Crane stance

Cobra stance beats Tiger stance

Controls Red fighter:

A: Move left, D: Move Right, W: Jump, Q or E: Change Stance, Spacebar: Attack

Controls Blue fighter:

Arrow left: Move left, Arrow right: Move Right, Arrow Up: Jump, Q or E: Change Stance, Spacebar: Attack, Shift or Num1: Change Stance, Arrow down: Attack
